The one accent color (clay) is reserved for primary actions and the FAB — everything else in InnerTable stays neutral. Four variants share one component.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary">Save to Want to Try</Button>
<Button variant="outline" size="sm">+ Add your take</Button>
<Button variant="ghost" size="sm">Filter & sort</Button>
<Button variant="fab" icon="+">Add a place</Button>
```
